Popular Games

There are many popular games on the Nintendo Switch. Some launch titles included 1-2 Switch, Breath of the Wild, Just Dance 2017, and Shovel Knight. Mario Kart 8 Deluxe was released not too long after the Switch's release date, and it is still one of the most popular Switch games along with Breath of the Wild.

Best-Selling games:

  1. Mario Kart 8 Deluxe (67.35 million copies sold)
  2. Animal Crossing New Horizons (47.44 million copies sold)
  3. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate (35.88 million copies sold)
  4. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild (32.62 million copies sold)
  5. Super Mario Odyssey (29.04 million copies sold)
  6. Pokemon Sword and Shield (26.60 million)
  7. Pokemon Scarlet and Violet (26.38 million)
  8. The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom
  9. Super Mario Party
  10. New Super Mario Bros. U Deluxe

Some other popular Switch games include Super Mario Odyssey, Minecraft, Splatoon 3, Bayonetta 2, and The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening.
